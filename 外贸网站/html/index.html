about US
Xiamen Fashion Knitting Co., Ltd
Xiamen Fashion Knitting Co., Ltd, established in 1992, located in Xiamen, China, is a lace manufacturer which specializes in lace R&D, production and sales. The factory premise is a 5-floor 1,000m2 building; with an operative surface of over 12,000m2. Xiamen Fashion has been recognized by international standard such as ISO9000 Certification and Oeko-Tex Standard 100 Certification.
